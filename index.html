About Me

Back in high school, I decided to try my hand at creating my own website and tumbled head first into the rabbit hole of coding and web development. Fast-forward to today, and I’ve had the privilege of building software for a start-up, a huge corporation, and an IT service management company.

Lately, my focus has been on pursuing my master's degree, a journey that has enriched my understanding of the software development process. As I've delved into the different areas of technology, I've come to appreciate their profound impact on different aspects of society. Throughout this experience, I've immersed myself in the dynamic tech industry, taking leadership and management roles in software development projects. Collaborating closely with both developers and clients, I've honed my skills in crafting tailor-made software solutions that truly resonate with users' needs.

When I’m not at the computer, I’m usually in the gym, reading, hanging out with my friends, or running around Melbourne searching for the next best cafe.
